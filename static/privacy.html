Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 31, 2026

TubeRank ("we", "us", or "our") is operated by Vladyslav Deriabin, based in the Czech Republic. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service at TubeRank.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Email address — provided voluntarily when activating a Pro subscription
  • IP address — collected automatically to enforce free-tier usage limits
  • Payment information — processed securely by Stripe; we do not store card details
  • Video topic input — the text you enter to generate SEO content (not stored after processing)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To verify your Pro subscription status via email
  • To enforce the free plan daily generation limit via IP address
  • To process payments through Stripe
  • To improve the quality of our AI-generated content

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Stripe — our payment processor (Stripe Privacy Policy)
  • Anthropic — our AI provider that processes your topic inputs (Anthropic Privacy Policy)

4. Data Retention

Email addresses linked to active subscriptions are retained for as long as the subscription is active. IP-based usage data resets daily and is not stored permanently.

5. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data
  • Data portability

To exercise these rights, contact us at tuberank.support@gmail.com.

6. Cookies

TubeRank uses localStorage (not cookies) to remember your theme preference and Pro activation status. No tracking cookies or third-party analytics are used.

7. Security

We use HTTPS encryption for all data transmission. Payment data is handled entirely by Stripe and never passes through our servers.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at tuberank.support@gmail.com.
