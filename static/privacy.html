⚖️3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your personal data under the following legal bases as defined in GDPR Article 6:

  • Contract performance (Art. 6(1)(b)): Processing your email and payment data to fulfil your Pro subscription
  • Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)): Processing IP addresses to prevent abuse of the free tier and protect the Service from misuse
  • Legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c)): Retaining payment records as required by applicable accounting and tax laws

🔍4. How We Use Your Data

  • Email: To verify your active Pro subscription when you enter it in the app. We do not send marketing emails.
  • IP address: To count and enforce the free-tier daily generation limit. The counter resets at midnight UTC and is not stored permanently.
  • Payment data: Processed entirely by Stripe. We receive only a customer ID and subscription status — never raw card details.
  • Topic inputs: Forwarded to the Anthropic Claude API to generate content. Not stored on our servers after processing.
  • YouTube URLs: Forwarded to the YouTube Data API v3 to retrieve public video tags. Not stored after processing.
We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party for marketing or advertising purposes.

🤝5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We share data with the following third-party service providers only to the extent necessary to operate the Service:

  • Anthropic, PBC (United States) — AI content generation
    Your topic inputs are sent to Anthropic's Claude API. Anthropic may process this data under their own privacy policy: anthropic.com/privacy
  • Stripe, Inc. (United States) — Payment processing
    Your email and payment card details are processed by Stripe. Stripe is PCI-DSS certified. Privacy policy: stripe.com/privacy
  • Google LLC (United States) — YouTube Data API v3
    YouTube video URLs submitted to the Tag Extractor are sent to Google's API. Privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy
  • Railway Corp. (United States) — Cloud hosting
    Our application and server logs are hosted on Railway's infrastructure. Privacy policy: railway.app/legal/privacy

All third-party providers are contractually required to protect your data and may only use it for the specific purposes we define.

🌍6. International Data Transfers

Our service providers (Anthropic, Stripe, Google, Railway) are based in the United States. When your data is transferred to the US, it is protected by appropriate safeguards, including Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission, and/or the providers' participation in equivalent frameworks.

🗓️7. Data Retention

  • Email addresses: Retained in Stripe for as long as your subscription is active, plus any period required by tax or accounting law (typically 5–7 years)
  • IP addresses: Used in-memory for daily rate limiting only. Not written to disk or retained beyond the server process lifecycle
  • Topic inputs / YouTube URLs: Not retained. Processed in real time and discarded
  • Payment records: Retained by Stripe in accordance with their data retention policies and applicable legal requirements

🍪8. Cookies and Local Storage

TubeRank does not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or any third-party analytics scripts (such as Google Analytics).

We use browser localStorage (not cookies) for the following purposes:

  • Theme preference: Remembers whether you prefer dark or light mode
  • Pro status: Stores a local flag (tuberank_pro = 'true') after you verify your Pro subscription via email, so you don't need to re-verify on every visit

This data is stored entirely on your device and is never transmitted to our servers. You can clear it at any time by clearing your browser's local storage.

🔒9. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data:

  • All data in transit is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Payment data is handled exclusively by Stripe and never passes through our servers
  • All API keys and secrets are stored as environment variables on Railway — never hardcoded in source code
  • We do not maintain a database of user accounts or passwords

No system is completely secure. If you suspect a security issue with our Service, please report it to tuberank.support@gmail.com.

👶10. Children's Privacy

TubeRank is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

✊11. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or Switzerland, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access (Art. 15): Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification (Art. 16): Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to erasure (Art. 17): Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten"), subject to legal retention requirements
  • Right to restriction (Art. 18): Request that we restrict processing of your data under certain conditions
  • Right to data portability (Art. 20): Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to object (Art. 21): Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to withdraw consent: Where processing is based on consent, withdraw it at any time without affecting prior processing

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at tuberank.support@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days. We may need to verify your identity before processing your request.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. In the Czech Republic, this is the Office for Personal Data Protection (ÚOOÚ).
